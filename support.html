Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a game?

Tap the "Create Game" button on the main screen, add your questions, and invite your partner to play!

How do I join a game?

Use the access code provided by your partner or click the link in the invitation email.

Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes! Your games are synced across all your devices when you're signed in.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your information.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Account settings in the app and select "Delete Account" or contact us at support@mrandmrs.tech
